DevBootcamp Chicago

Technical and cultural student blogs

February to June, 2015

As part of being a student at the "computer programming crash course" known as DevBootcamp, I was required to maintain two weekly blogs during my time there; one a technical blog where I re-explained things I had learned that week specifically to a non-technical audience, and the other a "cultural" blog, where I discussed the emotions I was going through while taking the classes and confronting various, sometimes intense challenges. Below is a list of all blog entries currently written and available here at this website. For those who are thinking of joining DevBootcamp and found my blog through a Google search on DBC advice, you can consider this a pretty realistic look at what you can expect to go through as a student yourself.
